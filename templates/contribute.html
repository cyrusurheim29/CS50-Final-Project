About Contributing

Equal Opportunities Boston is a completely open access initiative, relying on robust socioeconomic data and regional information that describes the Greater Boston area. We appreciate any and all contributions to this project, whether it be through updates on the availability of individual opportunities and the emergence of new databases in the future, or any other information or suggestion that may arise. Contact us here if you are interested in getting involved!
